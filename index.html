About

I create colourful porcelain jewellery, handmade at home in Lancashire.

A primary school teacher by trade, I took a career break to look after my daughter, and it was signing up for a ceramics adult education class in 2018 that allowed me to discover clay. I quickly fell in love and it really helped me to feel like me again! Last summer, I was lucky enough to stumble upon a lady selling a second hand kiln and began to develop my own home studio. This is where I now produce all my work.


All my jewellery is made from light porcelain, coloured throughout to achieve a vibrant effect. I use gold lustre accents to create abstract patterns and add a bit of sparkle. The whole process requires three separate firings in my little kiln. I work in very small batches so many pieces are completely unique. Inspired by the seasons, new colour collections are added throughout the year.
